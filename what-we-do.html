Frequently Asked Questions

What programs does NCORE offer?

NCORE offers Cyber Jagrukta, Digital Detox programs, Junior NCORE Centers, College MoU programs, NCORE Clubs, Hackathons/CTFs, Workshops & Training, and Community Outreach initiatives.

How can a college sign an MoU?

Colleges can submit a collaboration request through our form. We will schedule a discussion to understand requirements and propose a customized MoU framework.

How can we start an NCORE Club?

Your college must have an active MoU with NCORE. Interested students can then form a club with faculty mentorship. NCORE provides guidelines and ongoing support.

Do you conduct school programs?

Yes, through our Junior NCORE Center initiative. Programs focus on age-appropriate cyber safety education for different grade levels.

Can CSR partners fund a program?

Yes. Corporate partners can sponsor programs as part of CSR initiatives. We offer structured engagement models with impact reporting and recognition opportunities.

Do you provide certifications / reports?

Yes. NCORE provides participation certificates and comprehensive reports documenting program outcomes, participant feedback, and impact metrics.
