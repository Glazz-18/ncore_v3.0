About the Program

NCORE Rehabilitation Centers deliver structured rehabilitation for cyber harm survivors by combining counseling, legal guidance, digital recovery, and long-term reintegration support. We work with institutions, CSR partners, and government stakeholders to establish centers that restore dignity and create sustainable pathways to recovery.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum requirement to open a center?
We typically require a committed local partner, suitable infrastructure, and alignment with our rehabilitation framework.
How long does the setup take?
Setup timelines vary by location and partner readiness, typically between 8‚Äì16 weeks.
Can district administrations collaborate?
Yes. We regularly collaborate with government departments for referrals, outreach, and infrastructure support.
Do you provide operational support?
NCORE provides operating protocols, training, and ongoing monitoring for center operations.
Can we sponsor only one center?
Absolutely. Sponsors can support individual centers with localized impact reporting.
How do you measure impact?
Impact is tracked through rehabilitation outcomes, reintegration metrics, and program reporting.
