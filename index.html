About Us

NCORE Foundation (Network for Cyber Online Rehabilitation & Empowerment) is India‚Äôs first initiative dedicated to holistic cyber rehabilitation.

We support people and communities affected by cybercrime through integrated care that brings together psychological healing, legal aid, digital literacy, and survivor-led empowerment. Through education, awareness, and collaboration with government, law enforcement, and civil society, we help survivors reclaim dignity and rebuild trust in the digital world.

Vision

Reform. Rebuild. Rise.

Mission
